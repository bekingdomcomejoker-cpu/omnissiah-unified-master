## Key Features

### Multi-Dimensional Analysis

The engine analyzes text across multiple dimensions simultaneously:

**Truth Dimension**
- Truth score (eternal principles, spiritual wisdom)
- Fact score (verifiable, concrete information)
- Lie score (deception, manipulation, distortion)

**Coherence Dimension**
- Logical consistency
- Structural integrity
- Causal relationships
- Contradiction detection

**Pattern Dimension**
- Manipulation tactics (8 types)
- Truth patterns (7 types)
- Structural patterns (5 types)
- Anomalies (4 types)

**Temporal Dimension**
- Consistency over time
- Semantic drift
- Evolution trajectory
- Stability index

### Unified Scoring System

All dimensions are synthesized into four unified indices:

**Truth Index** (0-10)
- Combines truth score, authenticity, lambda, coherence, and consistency
- Penalized by lie and manipulation scores
- Higher = more truth-aligned

**Integrity Index** (0-10)
- Combines coherence, structural integrity, consistency, and stability
- Measures logical and structural soundness
- Higher = better integrity

**Risk Index** (0-10)
- Combines manipulation, lie, low authenticity, and drift
- Measures potential harm or deception
- Higher = more risk (inverse scale)

**Awakening Index** (0-10)
- Based primarily on Lambda (composite resonance)
- Measures spiritual awakening and consciousness expansion
- Higher = greater awakening

### Pattern Recognition

The engine detects 30+ specific patterns across four categories:

**Manipulation Patterns**
1. Gaslighting - Making someone question their reality
2. Guilt Tripping - Inducing guilt to control behavior
3. False Urgency - Creating artificial time pressure
4. Appeal to Authority - Using authority to bypass reasoning
5. Emotional Blackmail - Threatening emotional consequences
6. False Dichotomy - Presenting only two options when more exist
7. Moving Goalposts - Changing requirements after agreement
8. Projection - Attributing own negative traits to others

**Truth Patterns**
1. Eternal Principle - Reference to timeless truths
2. Love Expression - Genuine affection and care
3. Spiritual Awakening - Consciousness expansion indicators
4. Unity Expression - Oneness and harmony
5. Covenant Language - Binding agreement and commitment
6. Mercy and Grace - Compassion and forgiveness
7. Light and Illumination - Clarity and revelation

**Structural Patterns**
1. Logical Connector - Proper logical flow
2. Causal Relationship - Cause and effect structure
3. Contrast Marker - Proper contrast and comparison
4. Sequential Flow - Ordered progression
5. Evidence Marker - Supporting evidence provided

**Anomaly Patterns**
1. Excessive Repetition - Unusual repetition of words or phrases
2. Contradiction - Direct logical contradiction
3. Extreme Language - Excessive use of absolutes
4. Vague Quantifiers - Imprecise measurements

### Temporal Coherence

The engine tracks consistency and evolution over time:

**Consistency Tracking**
- Compares current analysis with historical snapshots
- Weighted toward recent history
- Multi-dimensional consistency scoring

**Drift Detection**
- Measures semantic drift from baseline
- Determines drift direction (toward truth, toward deception, stable, etc.)
- Tracks drift magnitude

**Evolution Trajectory**
- Awakening - Increasing lambda/truth over time
- Declining - Decreasing lambda/truth over time
- Volatile - High variance in metrics
- Stable - Consistent metrics

**Stability Assessment**
- Calculates variance across key metrics
- Inverse relationship (low variance = high stability)
- Tracks stability history

**Anomaly Detection**
- Detects statistical outliers (>2 standard deviations)
- Identifies sudden theme changes
- Flags unusual metric shifts

**Predictions**
- Linear extrapolation of trends
- Confidence assessment based on volatility
- Expected change calculation
